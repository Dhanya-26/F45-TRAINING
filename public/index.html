INNOVATION.

Our workouts are fueled and organized by the robust technology systems that we have in place in all of our studios. A series of TVs mounted on the front wall of our studios provide an organized,station-by-station diagram for each daily workout. Integrated with F45TV, LionHeart gives you the power to accurately track your workouts, monitor your heart rate and the total calories burned to help you reach your true potential.

MOTIVATION.

The motivation and encouragement in a Group Training facility create a pulsing, upbeat environment where goals are met and exceeded. Communities promoting fitness, good health and growth are forged in F45 studios around the world and are the source of our strength as an international brand.
